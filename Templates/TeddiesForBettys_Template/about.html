=========================ABOUT US=================================================

Potted is the vision of Mary Gray, a former art director and set decorator, and Annette Goliti Gutierrez, a one-time screenwriter with a love for  renovating homes and gardens.

In 2004, we opened what we envisioned to be a mecca for like-minded, design-loving, garden enthusiasts – people like us who wanted to shop for the outdoor area of their homes with the same choices and style they have for their interiors. As we’ve evolved, we realized that what sets us apart is our continued desire to create our own products.  This blend of original pieces coupled with our love of seeking out other unique artisans has helped us to develop our diverse style – from kitsch to cottage and vintage to modern.  And so, after 10 amazing years, Potted has become an outdoor-lifestyle destination.

If you’re in Los Angeles, please come visit us in our Los Feliz/Atwater Village store.

We’d love to inspire you with our Potted Style.

Potted.  We design.  We curate.  We have fun.

=========================Policy FAQs=================================================

What forms of payment does Potted accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express.

What is Potted's return policy?

We will accept returns on purchases with either a store credit or exchange within 10 days from delivery date. Items must be returned in their original condition with the original packing, tags, etc. including a copy of the purchase receipt. Shipping costs from Potted are not refundable. Cost for a return shipment is the responsibility of the customer unless otherwise stated.

 

Exception to return policy - ALL FOUNTAINS, FIRE PITS OR FURNITURE. These are final sale unless it is determined there is a manufacturer's or shipping issue.

Potted will always work with you to resolve any shipping or manufacturer's mistakes however it is the customer's responsibility to inspect items upon delivery.

How do I do a return?

Email us at info@pottedstore.com with the information of the item(s) to be returned and the reason for the return. You will receive an email from Potted within 7 days of your request with instructions. If you received a gift from the Pottedstore website, your gift will be refunded for a store credit.

Damage claims?

We pack and inspect our merchandise carefully to insure a safe delivery. If however damage does occur, please contact us immediately via email at info@pottedstore.com within 5 days of receipt to arrange for a replacement. UPS and FedEx usually require an inspection in the event of damage, so please hold on to all packing materials and the damaged item until damage assessment is resolved. Please, inspect all deliveries carefully upon arrival.

Shipping

Currently, we are shipping to addresses within the Continental USA only. All orders are shipped either UPS ground or FedEx ground (which averages 4 to 5 business days) unless other arrangements have been made. In stock orders typically ship within 2-3 business days. If we cannot ship within 2-3 days we will inform you with new shipment date via email. Custom orders and drop ship items have a longer lead time and specifics are noted in the item description. Shipping charges are based on purchase amounts unless otherwise noted in the product description.

Faster shipping?

If you want faster shipping, please contact us at 323-665-3801 and we will provide you with expedited shipping options.

Tracking an order

An email will be sent to you confirming shipment with tracking information.

At Potted, we recycle shipping materials in an effort to reduce waste.

Cancellations & Backorders

Once an order is processed and packaged a 15% cancellation fee will be incurred if you choose to cancel.  For items that are made-to-order from a third party or special-ordered, the cancellation fee is 40%. If an item is out of stock, you will be notified via email and the shipment will not be backordered unless approved by you, the customer. We cannot be held responsible for any errors or delays by the manufacturer. We work conscientiously to make sure our customers receive the best possible customer service available.

Tax

California sales tax will be added to your order if we are shipping within California.

Security

The Potted website features secured payments and is hosted on a server featuring SSL (secure socket layer). SSL is a protocol that allows a web browser and a web server to communicate securely by encrypting all information sent.

=========================Privacy=================================================

Your Privacy

Thank you for shopping at pottedstore.com. Ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information is very important to us. Our privacy and security policy outlines the information we collect about you, how and why we use the information and the choices you have to restrict our usage of this information.

Collected Information

Personal information you provide to us while creating or updating an account and at checkout (ie: name, address, telephone number, email address, billing address, ship-to address and credit card information) is maintained in private files on our secure web server and on our internal systems. We also use cookies* to collect product and purchase information from the pages you access or visit. This information may be combined with other identifiable personal information (such as demographic and past purchase information) from our records and other sources. We may use this combined information for future marketing initiatives and to help us improve our website presentation and your overall shopping experience.

*Cookies are bits of information that are automatically stored on your computer. Cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information such as your name, address or any financial information. If you do not wish to have cookies on your system, you can set your browser preferences to disable them.

Our Email List

We offer those who sign-up for our email list advanced notice of sales, new merchandise and other Potted news. Email addresses collected at pottedstore.com are only used internally. We respect your privacy rights and will not sell or rent your email address to other companies.

If you would like to unsubscribe from our email list, please send an email to info@pottedstore.com. We will remove your name from our email list as soon as possible. Please recognize that you may receive another email before we are able to remove you.

Secure Shopping

Orders placed on our site are encrypted using SSL technology*, so you can rest assured that your transactions are protected. With SSL implemented on both the client and server, information sent via the Internet can be trusted to arrive privately and unaltered to our server. In order for SSL security to work, you must use an SSL enabled browser. Most browsers, version 3.x and later, support SSL.
